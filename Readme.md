MedAssist AI
 An AI-powered multilingual virtual healthcare assistant that helps patients manage medications, appointments, and health queries while keeping caregivers and healthcare providers connected.

Overview
MedAssist AI is designed to bridge the gap between hospital care and home care by providing continuous healthcare support. It assists patients with medicine reminders, appointment tracking, AI-powered health guidance, and multilingual communication.
The platform is especially helpful for elderly patients and people with chronic diseases by improving medication adherence and reducing missed appointments. 1
